Our Specialized Services

Evidence-based programs designed to support individuals with autism at every stage of life

Early Intervention

Specialized programs for children ages 18 months to 5 years focusing on communication, social skills, and developmental milestones.

School Support

Collaboration with educational institutions to develop IEPs, provide classroom support, and ensure academic success.

Social Skills Training

Structured programs to develop communication, relationship-building, and social interaction skills in various settings.

Family Support

Resources, training, and counseling for parents and caregivers to support their loved ones effectively.

Transition Planning

Support for adolescents and young adults transitioning to adulthood, including vocational training and independent living skills.

Sensory Integration

Therapy programs designed to help individuals process and respond to sensory information in their environment.

About Our Center

The Autism Care Center was established in 2010 with a mission to provide comprehensive, evidence-based support for individuals with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) and their families.

Our team of certified specialists, therapists, and educators work collaboratively to create personalized support plans that address the unique strengths and challenges of each individual.
